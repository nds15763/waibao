KingDot Privacy Policy

KingDot is committed to safeguarding your data and ensuring the highest standards of privacy protection. When using KingDot Products, we do not track, monitor, record, or store your traffic data, including browsing history, traffic destinations, data content, or search preferences.

Additionally, KingDot does not store connection logs. This means we do not maintain any records of your IP address, connection timestamps, or session durations. We do not access or associate your payment details with any activity conducted within KingDot Products. Our services are designed to protect your privacy, and we place significant emphasis on ensuring the security and confidentiality of your data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Scope of the Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all KingDot services, including our suite of applications and this website. It does not apply to third-party websites, products, or services, even if they link to or from our platform. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services before sharing your information. By using KingDot's website or services, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy. If you disagree, please refrain from using our services.

Data Collection

KingDot Products support anonymous use, and registration or submission of personal information is not required. We do not record personal information.

Anonymous Information ("Non-personal Data")

We may collect and store non-personal data during your interactions with KingDot Products. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • - System data (e.g., mobile/desktop device properties, OS version, preferred language)
  • - Metadata (e.g., date and time of access, Google Advertising ID, connectivity status, mobile service provider)

Non-personal data is anonymized and cannot be linked to individual users. Additionally, KingDot does not collect information about your activities within the application, such as browsing history, VPN traffic destinations, DNS queries, or IP addresses. This data is used to monitor and improve service quality and user experience.

At your discretion, you may enable VPN connection diagnostics to allow app data to be shared with our customer support team. This helps resolve technical issues and improve performance. Connection logs may be included when submitting support tickets to aid in debugging.

How We Collect Non-personal Data

KingDot Products

To optimize our global infrastructure, we collect statistical data to analyze user trends and demand. Examples include:

  • - Connection Attempts: To monitor service requests based on time intervals, country of origin (excluding source IP addresses), app version, etc.
  • - Successful Connections: To track successful VPN connections and identify potential network issues, peak usage times, and infrastructure needs.

Google Firebase

KingDot uses Google Firebase, a secure third-party service, to improve product reliability. For more details, please refer to Firebase's Privacy Policy.

Service Performance and No-Logs Policy

KingDot adheres to a strict no-logs policy, ensuring we do not track or store user activities within the VPN tunnel, including:

  • - Browsing history, traffic destinations, or search preferences
  • - IP addresses or DNS queries
  • - Data content

We do not associate users with specific online activities, ensuring complete anonymity.

Children's Privacy

You must be at least 16 years old and legally competent to use KingDot Services. If you are under 18, we require parental or guardian consent. KingDot does not knowingly collect or market data to children under the age of 16.

Data Retention

KingDot retains collected information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Inaccurate or incomplete data may be rectified or removed at our discretion.

Policy Updates

KingDot may update this Privacy Policy at its sole discretion. Changes take effect immediately upon publication. We encourage users to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how their data is collected, used, and protected. The "Last Modified" date reflects the most recent changes. Continued use of KingDot services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
